2024-07-30

I've been going outside a lot recently, whether it be to the CLAMP exhibition (pic related), the graves of relatives, or a friend's DJ event.
The CLAMP exhibition was amazing, with so many original color illustrations and manga panels on show. I was able to see artwork from my favourite CLAMP works like Cardcaptor Sakura, Chobits, and Kobato, along with a few illustrations from related works like Code Geass.
Going to the cemetery was a relaxing experience, talking with relatives about the time spent with (recently) deceased family members. The cemetery itself was beautiful and quiet, with a relaxing atmosphere, even in the Tokyo summer.
I'd never been to a DJ event before, and although it was in a relatively small club, the flashing lights and the loud music was very overstimulating. I really enjoyed it, and I'd love to go again.
